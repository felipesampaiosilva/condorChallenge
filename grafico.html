Water Cycle

Evaporation

Water from oceans, lakes, and rivers turns into vapor and rises into the air.

Condensation

Water vapor cools down and forms clouds in the atmosphere.

Precipitation

Clouds release water as rain, snow, or other types of precipitation.

Collection

Water is collected in oceans, lakes, rivers, and underground reservoirs, starting the cycle again.
